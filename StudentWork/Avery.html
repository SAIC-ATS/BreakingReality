Awaken

Description:

As I was brainstorming my ideal post-pandemic reality, I found myself thinking about all the issues in our present reality. Ones that we will have to face, even after we overcome the coronavirus. Things like corrupt politicians, the crushing machine of capitalism, racism, police brutality, global warming, people valuing money over human life and more. So, my hope for our post-pandemic reality is that more people become aware of these issues, and become awake enough to realize that they can help solve them...That, doing the right thing is beautiful.
