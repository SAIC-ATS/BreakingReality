Catie Burrill

Zee Horse Ballet

Description:


DO YOU EVER WISH THAT YOU WERE A HORSE DANCING WILD AND FREE INTO THE GREAT OBLIVION?

BEHOLD, YOU CAN!

JOIN OUR VIRTUAL ENSEMBLE BALLET AND WIGGLE WITH US.


ZEE HORSE BALLET serves as a virtual accessory to ZEE HORSE puppet, a large scale fiber piece I created for a screen printing class during the quarantine. I used the fabrics that I screen printed in that class to construct the horse. Using the same pattern from the screen prints, I illustrated my digital horses. This was not my original idea but developed through my time being isolated at home. The first couple of weeks were very challenging for me and I felt super stir crazy and trapped, similar to the dynamic movements of the little horses trapped within ZEE HORSE or on the ZEE HORSE BALLET effect. Being in quarantine, I started thinking, what do I actually want to make? What do I really want to come alive and look at everyday? I always came back to this dream I have. I want to create a roadside attraction, where I will entertain the masses with miniature horses and have little races, and little rodeos. I want to call it ZEE WEE RANCH. My final projects (ZEE HORSE and ZEE HORSE BALLET) are an ode to this idea, to the utopic place I wish to call home. As difficult as this has been, I am thankful for this time away from school to remind me who I am and what I want to create rather than what my assignments are.


ZEE HORSE

Screen printed fabrics, machine sewn appliquéd mini-horses, velvet, rhinestones, foil, thread, painted dowel rods.
