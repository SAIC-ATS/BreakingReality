Brigid O'Neil

Future Nature

Description:

This filter is based on my idea of what I believe the post-pandemic future holds for the environment: Soon after the world went into quarantine, people noticed how nature immediately began to repair itself. People began posting online about how “WE are the virus”, and several memes followed. In creating this, I emphasize the power of man in regard to our role in the ecosystem.


To experience this filter:

If you are able, stand in the middle of an open space, and view the filter as you turn around.
