Description:

Low-budget (no-budget) amateur filmmaking meets low-budget amateur AR effects.


This video came out of a genuine desire to play and let go of the stuffy fine-art-standards I tend to hold myself up to while making art at SAIC. What better way to let go of that than to go out and shoot at, what appears to everybody else around me, seemingly nothing? Itâ€™s an absolutely dorky video but I hope those who watch can enjoy my foolishness while also contemplating what it would be like to navigate this world with augmented reality or to explore it through a virtual self.


The characters were provided and animated through Mixamo. From there, I uploaded the animated characters to Spark AR and applied some basic user controls to make effects I could use on my phone. I uploaded my effects through the Spark AR Player app on my iPhone and filmed through that. The video was edited through iMovie.
